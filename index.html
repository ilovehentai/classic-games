How to Play

Press ENTER to start the game. First to 5 points wins!

Move your paddle to hit the ball back to your opponent.

The ball speeds up when hit near the paddle edges!

Wars Mode: Shoot lasers to stun your opponent for 0.5 seconds!
One laser per screen - wait for it to go off-screen before shooting again.

Player 1 (Left)

Q - Move Up
A - Move Down
S - Shoot Laser

Player 2 (Right)

P - Move Up
L - Move Down
K - Shoot Laser

Computer (Right)

AI Controlled
